Daal Palak (Lentils and Spinach)

Submitted by Sarah Ijaz

Ingredients

  • Red Lentils: 2 cups
  • Chopped Spinach: 1 cup
  • Red chili powder: 1 tsp
  • Turmeric: 1 tsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste: 2 tsp
  • Chopped tomato: 1 medium

  • Tadka (Tempering)

  • Cumin seeds 1 tbsp
  • Oil 1/4th cup
  • Onion 1
  • Chopped green chillies: 2
  • Chopped, fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash lentils
  2. Add lentils and spinach into a pot with enough water to cover them and cook on low flame.
  3. Add red chili powder, turmeric, salt, ginger garlic paste, tomatoes and mix well. Cook for 15 minutes on medium flame.
  4. While the lentils are coooking: In a pan, heat oil, add onion and cumin seeds, and fry until golden brown.
  5. Turn the flame off from the lentils, and while they are still hot, pur the hot oil and fried onions over the top.
  6. Finally, garnish with green chillies and coriander.
