Hungarian Goulash

Submitted by Lara Molnar

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large or two medium onions, chopped
  • 4 large red peppers, chopped into bite size pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed or chopped finely
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 400g tin tomatoes sauce
  • chicken or vegetable stock

Method

  1. Sweat the onions in the olive oil. When starting to soften add the peppers and garlic. (You could add some cubes of potato at this stage if you want a more filling dish)
  2. Once the peppers have softened add the paprika and stir around in the oil for 1 minute (making sure heat is not too high so that it does not catch)
  3. Add the tin of tomatoes and the stock - just enough to barely cover the vegetables.
  4. Cook over a medium heat until vegetables are soft and sauce has thickened - 10-15 mins
  5. Serve with a sprinkling of fresh mint

(warning - it stays super hot, so wait a while before eating)
