Squeezing the Juice from a Lemon/Lime

Submitted by Iqrah Tauhid

Squeezing the juice from a lemon or a lime can be quite the difficult task, resulting in a sore hand
and very little juice.

Method

  1. Take a lemon or lime and put it on a flat surface.
  2. Using the palm of your hand, push down on the fruit lightly and roll it back and forth
  3. Keep rolling and pushing down until the firm fruit becomes mushy.
  4. Cut the lemon or lime in half and squeeze the juice out easily!
